Get to know me

My name is Silje, and I am a UX designer and frontend developer living in Sandnes, Norway.

I have always adored storytelling, visual work, creative problem-solving and helping people, and love to combine my design and code skills with my media background.

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in frontend development, to add to my UX abilities. When I am working on a problem I am stubborn in the best way: I will not give up until something is fixed or improved.

The drive for improvement is important for me, as I thrive on sharpening my skills and learning new tools and techniques that I can use in my work or spare time.

Outside of career and academics, I enjoy knitting, playing the Sims 4, weight-lifting, listening to music, filming and photographing.

  • Adobe Premiere, Photoshop and Illustrator
